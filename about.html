About Us

The Ninja Grocer is originally a concept back when i was in college for faster grocery transactions back in my hometown of Magdalena Laguna. Food accesabilityt was one of our major problems there since we live about 3-4 kilometeres away from the nearest grocery store and wetmarket. So here in Ninja Grocer, we always want the best for you, We offer fresh and 100% organic food and also have the power to choose between pick and free delivery.

Mission

To aid consumers on having easier access to healtthy and organic foods. Also in picking healthier options rather than the less healthier one

Vision

To Contribute on making the consumers life a little easier and making grocery shoping convient for them when they don't have the time nor the energy to step out.
